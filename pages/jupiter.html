June , 2006




Two images taken several days apart. The red spots are in nearly the same position both days and changes in the bands and other spots can be seen. Also, the images were taken with different camera resolution settings. There is very little difference in the final images because as usual, the amount of detail recorded is limited by the seeing. The frames recorded at 320x240 resolution would not have been compressed as much as the frames recorded at 640x480.
